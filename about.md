# About Us

Science AL!VE is a **STEAM** (Science, Technology, Engineering, Art & Design, Math) Outreach Organization, operating out of **Simon Fraser University's Faculty of Applied Sciences**.

Our mission is to engage youths from K-12 with hands-on programs to build skills and confidence in STEAM related fields. 

You can read more about us at [our official website](https://sciencealive.ca/about-science-alve).
