## Resources
If you want to continue on your web dev journey here are some more tools and resources to keep you going.

[CodePen](https://codepen.io/) is a site where you can build websites using your browser. The site also has monthly challenges and a big community to get inspiration from.

[MDN Ressource for Web Devs](https://codepen.io/) will have all the answers to any questions you might have about web development. They also have a pretty learning path to help you continue your web dev journey.

[Science AL!VE](https://sciencealive.ca/) lastly it's us! If you enjoy our workshops, please signup for our newsletter to hear about upcoming workshops!

We hope you enjoyed this workshop and wish you the best on your coding journey.
